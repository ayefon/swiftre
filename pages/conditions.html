Conditions

Conditions define when a rule fires. They compare field values against literals, check field presence, and can be combined with logical operators.

Equality

OperatorExample
ISWHEN status IS "active"
IS NOTWHEN status IS NOT "blocked"

Numeric Comparisons

OperatorExample
IS GREATER THANWHEN age IS GREATER THAN 18
IS GREATER THAN OR EQUAL TOWHEN score IS GREATER THAN OR EQUAL TO 90
IS LESS THANWHEN quantity IS LESS THAN 1
IS LESS THAN OR EQUAL TOWHEN balance IS LESS THAN OR EQUAL TO 0
IS AT LEASTWHEN orderTotal IS AT LEAST 50
IS AT MOSTWHEN retries IS AT MOST 3
IS EQUAL TOWHEN count IS EQUAL TO 0

Cross-type comparison: Integers and doubles are compared numerically. WHEN count IS GREATER THAN 2.5 works even if count is an integer.

String Operators

OperatorExample
CONTAINSWHEN email CONTAINS "@"
STARTS WITHWHEN phone STARTS WITH "+1"
ENDS WITHWHEN filename ENDS WITH ".pdf"
MATCHESWHEN zipCode MATCHES "^[0-9]{5}$"

Case Sensitivity

String comparisons are case-sensitive by default. Add IGNORING CASE to make them case-insensitive:

WHEN department CONTAINS "engineering" IGNORING CASE
WHEN country IS "usa" IGNORING CASE

Presence Checks

IS SETWHEN email IS SETField exists and is not null
IS NOT SETWHEN phone IS NOT SETField is missing or null

Date Comparisons

OperatorExample
IS BEFOREWHEN expiryDate IS BEFORE TODAY
IS AFTERWHEN startDate IS AFTER DATE "2025-01-01"
IS ON OR BEFOREWHEN deadline IS ON OR BEFORE TOMORROW
IS ON OR AFTERWHEN hireDate IS ON OR AFTER 30 DAYS AGO

See Dates for date arithmetic and difference expressions in conditions.

Logical Combinators

Combine multiple conditions with AND, OR, and NOT. Use parentheses for grouping:

AND

WHEN age IS SET AND age IS GREATER THAN 18

OR

WHEN status IS "expired" OR status IS "cancelled"

NOT

WHEN NOT (status IS "active")

Grouping with Parentheses

WHEN (status IS "active" OR status IS "pending")
  AND age IS GREATER THAN 18

Date Difference in Conditions

Compute the span between two dates and compare the result as an integer:

WHEN DAYS BETWEEN startDate AND endDate IS GREATER THAN 14
WHEN MONTHS BETWEEN hireDate AND TODAY IS AT LEAST 6
WHEN YEARS BETWEEN birthDate AND TODAY IS LESS THAN 18

See Dates for full date difference documentation.

Date Arithmetic in Conditions

Add or subtract durations from dates or fields:

WHEN expiryDate IS BEFORE TODAY + 30 DAYS
WHEN `contract.start` IS AFTER 6 MONTHS AGO
